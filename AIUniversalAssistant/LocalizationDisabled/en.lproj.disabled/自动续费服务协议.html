I. Service Description

1. Auto-Renewal Service

Auto-renewal service means that after you subscribe to membership service, the system will automatically renew your membership before it expires to ensure that your membership service continues uninterrupted.

2. Subscription Types

This application provides the following subscription types:

  • Weekly Membership: Subscription cycle is 7 days
  • Monthly Membership: Subscription cycle is 30 days
  • Annual Membership: Subscription cycle is 365 days
  • Lifetime Membership: One-time purchase, permanently valid (not applicable to auto-renewal)

3. Renewal Rules

  • The renewal cycle is the same as the subscription cycle you choose

  • We will notify you through the App Store before price adjustments

II. Billing Description

1. Billing Time

  • Auto-renewal will charge within 24 hours before your membership expires

  • We cannot control the exact billing time

2. Billing Method

  • Billing will be processed through the payment method bound to your Apple ID
  • Billing is processed by Apple App Store, and we cannot directly process billing
  • If billing fails, your membership service will terminate after expiration

3. Successful Renewal

  • After successful billing, your membership period will be automatically extended by one cycle
  • You will continue to enjoy all membership benefits
  • If a word pack was promised when subscribing, it will not be given again upon renewal

III. Cancel Renewal

1. Cancellation Method

You can cancel auto-renewal through the following methods:

  • iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Find "AI Writing Meow" → Cancel Subscription
  • Or cancel through App Store account settings

2. Cancellation Rules

  • You can cancel the auto-renewal service at any time without prior notice
  • After cancellation, the current membership period remains valid, and you can continue to use it until expiration
  • After expiration, your membership service will automatically terminate and will not be renewed
  • The cancellation takes effect immediately but will not affect the current paid membership period

3. Cancellation Restrictions

  • Cancellation must be completed at least 24 hours before the next billing
  • If you cancel within 24 hours before the next billing, the current billing may have already been initiated and cannot be cancelled
  • We recommend canceling in advance to avoid unnecessary charges

IV. Subscription Restoration

1. Restoration Method

If your subscription is interrupted due to billing failure or other reasons, you can restore it through the "Restore Subscription" function in this application.

2. Restoration Rules

  • Subscription restoration is based on your Apple ID
