Privacy Policy

We value your privacy very much. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information. Before using this application, please read and understand all the content of this Privacy Policy carefully.

I. Information Collection

We may collect the following information:

1. User Content Information

  • Writing content and prompts you input
  • Documents and creation records you create
  • Text content generated by AI functions you use

2. Account and Subscription Information

  • Apple ID (managed by Apple, we do not collect directly)
  • Subscription status and purchase records (through Apple IAP)
  • Word pack purchase and usage records
  • VIP membership status and expiration time

3. Usage Data

  • Application usage frequency and duration
  • Function usage and preference settings
  • Error logs and performance data
  • Advertising display and click data

4. Device Information

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Device unique identifier (IDFA, requires your authorization)
  • Network status and IP address
  • Application version number

5. Location Information

We do not collect your precise location information.

II. Information Use Purposes

The information we collect will be used for the following purposes:

1. Providing Services

  • Provide AI writing functions (continuation, rewriting, expansion, translation, etc.)
  • Manage subscription and word pack services
  • Synchronize data to iCloud
  • Restore subscriptions and purchase records

2. Improving Services

  • Analyze usage data to optimize application performance
  • Fix errors and improve user experience
  • Develop new features and functions

3. Personalized Experience

  • Provide personalized recommendations based on your usage habits
  • Optimize the accuracy of AI-generated content
  • Customize interface and function layout

4. Advertising Services

  • Display personalized advertisements (requires your IDFA authorization)
  • Analyze advertising effectiveness
  • Comply with advertising service providers' compliance requirements

5. Security and Compliance

  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Protect application and service security
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Respond to legitimate requirements of regulatory agencies

III. Information Storage and Protection

1. Storage Location

  • Local storage: Your writing content and creation records are stored locally on the device
  • iCloud storage: Word pack data is synchronized through Apple iCloud (requires your enablement)
  • Keychain storage: Sensitive information such as subscription status is stored in the device Keychain

2. Storage Period

  • Your writing content: Stored locally, deleted when the application is deleted
  • Subscription information: Stored during the subscription validity period, only historical records retained after expiration
  • Usage data: Retained anonymously for statistical analysis

3. Security Measures

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption: Sensitive data is stored encrypted
  • Access control: Limit access permissions to user data
  • Secure transmission: Use HTTPS and other secure protocols to transmit data
  • Regular audits: Regular security audits and vulnerability detection

If a merger, acquisition or asset transfer occurs, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

4. We Will Not

V. Your Rights

1. Right to Access

You have the right to know what personal information we collect about you and how we use this information. You can view your subscription status, word pack usage and other information in the application.

2. Right to Correct

If your personal information is incorrect, you have the right to request correction. You can update your preference settings in the application.

3. Right to Delete

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. You can delete through the following methods:

  • Delete application: Deleting the application will delete all locally stored data
  • Delete creation records: Delete your creation records in the application
  • Contact us: Request data deletion through the "Contact Us" function in the application

4. Withdraw Consent

  • IDFA authorization: You can withdraw IDFA authorization in iPhone Settings → Privacy → Tracking
