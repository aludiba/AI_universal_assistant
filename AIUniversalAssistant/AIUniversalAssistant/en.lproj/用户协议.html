I. Service Description

1. Application Introduction

AI Writing Meow is an intelligent writing assistant application based on artificial intelligence technology, providing you with the following core functions:

  • Creation Records: View and manage your creation history

2. Account System

This application uses Apple ID as the user identity identifier, and no separate account registration is required. Through Apple ID, you can make in-app purchases, restore purchased subscriptions and word packs, and synchronize word pack data between different devices.

II. Subscription and Purchase Services

1. Subscription Service

This application provides subscription types such as weekly membership, monthly membership, annual membership, and lifetime membership. The subscription service adopts an automatic renewal mechanism. Within 24 hours before the subscription expires, the system will automatically charge from your Apple ID account. You can cancel automatic renewal at any time in iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

2. Subscription Gift

When you first subscribe to any membership plan, you will receive a one-time gift of 500,000 words word pack for AI creation services. The gifted word pack is combined with VIP gifted words.

3. Word Pack Service

This application provides various word packs such as 500,000 words, 2,000,000 words, and 6,000,000 words. The word pack is valid for 90 days, calculated from the time of successful purchase. Each time you use the AI writing function, the word consumption = input text word count + generated text word count. Consumption order: VIP gifted words are consumed first, followed by purchased word packs.

4. Word Count Statistics Rules

One Chinese character, English letter, number, punctuation or space counts as 1 word.

6. Subscription Restoration

You can restore purchased subscriptions through the "Restore Subscription" function in this application. Subscription restoration is based on your Apple ID and can be used on all devices under the same Apple ID.

III. Data Synchronization Service

1. iCloud Synchronization

This application realizes cross-device synchronization of word pack data through Apple iCloud. All devices under the same Apple ID can automatically synchronize word pack data. If iCloud is not enabled, data will only be stored on the local device.
